Where Art Meets

Born from a passion for the extraordinary, Aroma was founded in 2012 by a collective of master perfumers who believed that fragrance is the most intimate form of art — invisible yet unforgettable, personal yet universal.

Every bottle in our collection represents hundreds of hours of meticulous craftsmanship, sourcing the world's rarest raw materials from Grasse to Kyoto, from the Bulgarian Rose valleys to the Cambodian forests of Oud.
