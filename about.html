Our Beginning

A Decade of
Olfactive Excellence

Aroma was born from a singular conviction: that fragrance is the most powerful form of personal expression known to humanity. Founded in 2012 by a trio of master perfumers who had each spent decades at the world's most prestigious fragrance houses, Aroma set out to create something entirely different.

We rejected the mass-market approach. We refused to compromise on ingredients. We chose instead to work with the rarest raw materials — real Bulgarian Rose, genuine Agarwood Oud, authentic Ambergris — even when it meant smaller batches and higher prices.

The result is a collection that speaks to those who know the difference. Each fragrance in our portfolio is the product of years of development, hundreds of trials, and an unwavering commitment to creating something truly extraordinary.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Uncompromising Quality

We source only the finest raw materials from around the world, refusing to substitute authentic ingredients for cheaper alternatives — regardless of cost.

Time-Honoured Craft

Each fragrance undergoes years of development. We believe the finest things cannot be rushed — they must be allowed to evolve and mature into perfection.

Sustainable Sourcing

We are committed to ethical and sustainable practices — working directly with farmers and cooperatives to ensure the longevity of the natural resources we depend upon.

How We Create

The Creative Process

01
Concept

Every fragrance begins with an emotion, a memory, or a place. Our perfumers spend weeks immersed in concept before touching a single ingredient.

02
Sourcing

We travel the world to source authentic ingredients — visiting rose farms in Bulgaria, Oud forests in Assam, and citrus groves in Calabria.

03
Composition

Hundreds of trials are conducted over months, sometimes years, until every note is in perfect harmony — a symphony for the senses.

04
Bottling

Each bottle is hand-filled, inspected, and packed with the same care given to the fragrance itself — because presentation is part of the experience.
